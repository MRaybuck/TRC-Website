Library By T.R.C.

The T.R.C. Library is a centralized, web-based collection of pedestrian and bicycling-related materials

and documents compiled by practitioners and researchers from across the United States and abroad.

These include sample pedestrian and bicycle plans and planning tools, design guidelines, research and safety studies,

articles and white papers, case studies, and links to presentations, videos, and other web resources.
